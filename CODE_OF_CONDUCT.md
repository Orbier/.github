# Orbier Code of Conduct!

## Our commitment
We welcome people of all identity, races and gender. Any hate towards any person for his Identity, Religion or race is NOT allowed.
We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, experience level, or background.

We disallow any kind of Racism, Homophobia, Sexism, Islamophobism etc.! Especially Sexism! 

## Expected Behaviour 

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Help others learn and grow

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexuality, race, or religion 
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Spamming or deliberately disrupting discussions
- Sharing private information without permission
- Political and Religious Beef!

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may take appropriate action in response to violations, including:
- Warning the offender
- Removing content
- Temporarily restricting access
- Permanently banning contributors

Please Contact through our Discord Server, or get touch with one of our project maintainers!
